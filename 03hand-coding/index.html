History of Jewelry making

Jewelry making has been around for thousands of years in many ancient civilizations. Archaeological evidence shows that the earliest forms of jewelry have started in different parts of the world.

Jewelry-making expanded in ancient Mesopotamia which is near Iran. Sumerian tombs have revealed jewelry made of gold, silver and gemstones.
